Download Instructions

Disclaimer: This application is in prototype version. It contains only the basic features. If you have any questions please provide feedback by clicking on “Tell Us!” link on the top menu bar.

Uninstall the current developmental version of C-threat from your phone
  1. Got to “Settings.”
  2. Click on “Applications.
  3. Click on “Application Manager.”
  4. Click on “C-THREAT.”
  5. Click on “Uninstall.”
  6. Go back to your home page on your Android Device.
Download the beta version of C-threat
  1. Click on “Android Download” link below.
  2. Once the download is complete, click on the “cthreat-beta-release.apk.”
  3. If your install is blocked, go to “Settings”, then go to “Lock screen and security”, toggle “Unknown sources” to “ON”, then check “Allow this installation only”, then press “OK”, then click on “INSTALL.”
  4. Finally click on “OPEN”. When C-THREAT is opened, register to use the app.
