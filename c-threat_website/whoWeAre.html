C-THREAT Partnership was established in early 2016 between two small technology companies, CBORGTECH LLC and New Horizon Investments and Consulting, Inc.

C-THREAT was conceived when our founders recognized the need for a platform that helps people handle threats and dangerous situations. A platform designed to help you at home, in your city or when traveling around the world. Our team was encouraged to work on a solution when we realized that easy-to-use emergency alerting systems aren't readily available, or that existing mobile technologies don't always get the message to the right people in an expeditious fashion.

We are eager to work closely with you and develop this simple and easy-to-use mobile application. C-THREAT enables your family, friends, neighbors and emergency responders in your community to post and receive alerts about developing emergency situations quickly and safely. Join us in this journey and help us bring to you the mobile application that will keep you informed and provide you peace of mind.
